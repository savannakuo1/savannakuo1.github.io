Book Readers

A middle-aged man

wearing a loose black T-shirt and olive green pants sits on
the subway, both feet firmly on the ground in his white Adidas sneakers. Square-framed
glasses sits on his face as he concentrates on a book, which lies open atop a plain
white tote bag on his lap. His right hand grips a black pen, poised to
take notes, while his left supports the book. He looks down on the book,
fully absorbed in the text before him.
