Sleepers

A young girl

stands quietly in a corner of the subway, dressed in a white
collared shirt under a black sweater, paired with a plaid long
skirt and white knee-high socks, along with sporty shoes. Her hair
is styled in a messy bun, and her lips are slightly downturned.
She rests her head against the train door, closing her eyes as if
in a deep sleep, with her hands crossed comfortably over her chest.
