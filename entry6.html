Coffee Drinkers

A lady

at 8 a.m. in a crisp white shirt and tailored pants is standing in the subway, her heels planted
firmly on the floor as the train sways. In one hand, she holds a steaming cup of coffee,
Her other hand grips a sleek leather bag slung over her shoulder. With her hair
pulled back into a low ponytail, her face has a thoughtful expression,
as if she is deep in thought.
